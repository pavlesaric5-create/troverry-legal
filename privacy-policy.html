Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 6, 2026

Introduction

TroVerry ("we," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our App.

The short version: We don't collect, store, or share any of your data. Everything stays on your device.

Information We Do NOT Collect

TroVerry is designed with privacy as the core principle. We do NOT collect:

  • ❌ Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • ❌ Device information or identifiers
  • ❌ Location data
  • ❌ Usage analytics or statistics
  • ❌ Crash reports or error logs
  • ❌ Photos or documents you attach to assets
  • ❌ Asset information (names, values, descriptions)
  • ❌ Any other data you enter into the App

How Your Data is Stored

Local Storage Only

  • All data is stored exclusively on your device using iOS secure storage
  • Photos and documents are stored in your device's file system
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • No cloud backup or sync (unless you use iCloud device backup)

Device Backup

  • If you back up your device to iCloud or iTunes, your TroVerry data is included
  • This is handled by iOS, not by our App
  • We have no access to your backups

Data Security

On Your Device

  • Data is protected by your device's security (Face ID, Touch ID, PIN)
  • The App requires biometric authentication to access
  • Files are stored in the App's secure container

We Cannot Access Your Data

  • We have no servers or databases
  • We cannot see what you store in the App
  • We cannot recover your data if you lose your device
  • We cannot provide cloud backup or sync

Third-Party Services

What We Don't Use

  • ❌ No analytics services (Google Analytics, Mixpanel, etc.)
  • ❌ No advertising networks
  • ❌ No crash reporting tools
  • ❌ No social media integrations
  • ❌ No tracking pixels or cookies

What We Do Use

  • ✅ Apple In-App Purchases: To process subscriptions and purchases
    • Handled entirely by Apple
    • We receive only basic transaction confirmations
    • See Apple's Privacy Policy for details
  • ✅ RevenueCat: For subscription management
    • Only processes anonymous purchase receipts
    • Does not access your asset data
    • See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy for details

Your Rights

Data Ownership

  • You own all data you enter into the App
  • You can export your data as PDF reports
  • You can delete the App and all data at any time

Data Deletion

  • Uninstalling the App deletes all local data
  • We have no data to delete on our end (we don't collect any)

Data Portability

  • Export your data as PDF insurance reports
  • Take screenshots of your assets
  • No centralized data to port

Children's Privacy

  • The App does not collect any data from anyone, including children
  • No personal information is collected from users of any age
  • The App is suitable for all ages (though focused on adults with valuable assets)

International Users

  • The App works the same way worldwide
  • All data stays on your device regardless of location
  • No data crosses borders because no data leaves your device

Changes to This Privacy Policy

  • We may update this policy to reflect App changes
  • Material changes will be notified through the App
  • Continued use constitutes acceptance of changes
  • Last updated date is shown at the top
